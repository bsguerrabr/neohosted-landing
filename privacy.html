Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2, 2026

NeoHosted ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our website and tools handle information when you visit neohosted.com and its subdomains.

Information We Do Not Collect

Our tools are designed to run entirely in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of the data you enter into our tools. For example:

  • Passwords created with our Password Generator are generated locally on your device and are never sent to any server.
  • Financial data entered into our Compound Interest Calculator is processed in your browser and is not stored or transmitted.

We do not require user accounts, and we do not collect names, email addresses, or any other personal identifiers through our tools.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to collect anonymous usage statistics, such as page views, referral sources, and general device information. This data helps us understand how our tools are used and how we can improve them. Google Analytics may use cookies to distinguish unique users. No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics.

For more information on how Google processes this data, please review Google's Privacy Policy.

Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on some of our pages. Google AdSense may use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our site or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Cookies

Our site may set cookies through the third-party services described above (Google Analytics and Google AdSense). We do not set any first-party cookies ourselves. You can control cookie behavior through your browser settings.

Third-Party Links

Our site may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can reach us at neohosted@proton.me.
